Strategic investment

AffMeta is RuleMaxx’s first strategic investment in audience intelligence.

A strategic entry into audience ownership, performance marketing and regulated digital entertainment. AffMeta is presented as a forward-looking platform built around audience onboarding, first-party data, consent-based engagement and partner activation.
